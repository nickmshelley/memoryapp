Time Offset

Set your time zone offset below.

The app decides which questions need reviewing based on the date. However, you may go to bed late and review after midnight, or you may miss a day. You can use this setting to give you extra time, or to make up a missed day.

The larger the number you enter, the earlier the app will think it is. In other words, the number you enter will be subtracted from the UTC time ({{ utcTime }} when you loaded this page). Notice that I do not currently take daylight savings into account (sorry, I'll get to it sometime).

Example: I live in Utah, so the number 6 or 7 (depending on daylight savings) would make the days start and end at midnight. However, I use an 8 hour offset so the app treats my day as starting and ending at 1 or 2 a.m. in case I review after midight.

If you miss a day and want to make it up, put in a number greater than 24 (plus your time zone offset). Remember to change it back when you're done, though.
